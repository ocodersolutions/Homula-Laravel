Sitemap submission

Getting Started ››

The sitemap is dynamically generated and can be accessed through sitemap.php. In order to automatically submit the updated sitemap to major search engines, you'll have to create a cron job on your host and specify pingSitemap.php in your daily cron. You'll have to specify the complete path to pingSitemap.php, for ex /home/www/YourSite.com/pingSitemap.php


Most of the hosting control panels allows you to create a cron job through their web interface. For more information on how to create a cron job, please refer to their documentation.
