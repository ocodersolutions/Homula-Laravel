Getting Started ››

This page displays a form to add a listing to your website. Listing status option is only visible to the admin. Members may add a residential or commercial listings of any type i.e available, wanted or on sale. This form automatically pulls information from the member profile. Though the member can change her contact information in this form. Some of the fields are marked as required so the form can't be submitted without those required values.

After adding the listing, the next page would show a form to upload pictures related to the listing. Members can easily add/edit/delete the listing or pictures anytime.
