Language Tags

Getting Started ››

This form lets you edit the existing language tags/translation. On one side English counterpart is shown and on another side, the translation in your default language. The English counterpart would help you identify what type of tag is it. For ex: 'Add Listing' tag is shown on the add listing form (on button at the bottom and at the top). It is recommended that you update the translation as translations shown there may not be perfect. Moreover try to reduce the length of translation as this would help preserve your website formatting.
