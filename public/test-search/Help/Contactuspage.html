Contact us page

Getting Started ›› Admin Options ››

This software is equipped with a contact us page on which you may display your address. This page also have a contact form using which people can contact you.


1) Contact address. The address specified here would be shown on the contact us page.


2) Contact email (for contact form): You'll receive emails on this email address when someone sends you a message through the contact form. It is recommended to use a different email than what you used in the SMTP settings.
