Adsense or other ads

Getting Started ›› Admin Options ››

This section helps you specify adsense or any advertisement code, if you want to monetize your website. The ads would be shown in the top (below top menu) and in the left sidebar, below login form.


1) Top link ad. (728 x 15): This ad will be displayed in the top right header of your website. Please make sure that the ad is not bigger than 728 x 15 px i.e 728px wide and 15 px height. For ex google adsense link ad is a good fit here.


2) Sidebar ad (not more than 250px wide): This ad will be displayed in the left sidebar, below login form. Please make sure that it is not wider than 250px as otherwise it would break the sidebar formatting.
